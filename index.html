A SHORT INTRODUCTION


Talking about wars, we often refer to the 1st and 2nd World War, the Cold War, etc. Humanity hopes that it doesnt see another big war one day. What we dont realise is that we are in the middle of one right now, except one side isn't attacking, but just defending. Since the invention of cultivation and the age of industrilisation, humanity has been shaping the world according to its needs. It was foreseen that nature would answer back. In this project we are going to see humanity's actions affecting nature and the effects of those actions that we are going to refer as nature's answer back.
